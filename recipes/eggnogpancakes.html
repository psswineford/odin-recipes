Eggnog Pancakes

The Best Pancakes you'll ever eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • Oil
  • Eggnog
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. In a medium sized bowl whisk together egg, oil, eggnog and vanilla. In a separate bowl combine flour, salt, sugar, baking powder, and cinnamon. Then, add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and combine.
